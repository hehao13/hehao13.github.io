Hao He 「何 昊」

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Multimedia Laboratory in the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My supervisors are Prof. Hongsheng Li and Prof. Xiaogang Wang.

Before CUHK, I received my Master's degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2022, my supervisor is Prof. Shiming Xiang. I obtained my Bachelor degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2019.

My research interests lie in the area of video generation and 3D Diffusion Models. Specifically, I am particularly interested in controlling the camera trajectories during the video genertion process and improving the 3D consistency of the generated videos.
